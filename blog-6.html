The Tokenization of Trust & Cryptocurrency Credibility

“A lack of transparency results in distrust and a deep sense of insecurity.”
-Dalai Lama

Our Private Pre-Sale recently went live and the team at Leap With Alice has been hitting the pavement, making connections, and reaching out to the education community. On the business end, there have been some major developments. Leap With Alice has formed a partnership with Bluebee Pals, an interactive plush learning companion linked to a FREE education app that is capable of pairing with any bluetooth application. Occupational, ABA, and Speech Therapists utilize Bluebee Pals as an effective motivational tool to engage in communication and learning. Each companion participates in repeating words, answering questions, and reading stories. Leap With Alice is partnering with Bluebee Pals to enhance engagement, in and beyond the classroom! Working together, we aim to create an assistive technology that peaks every child’s interest on an emotional and intellectual level.

As Bluebee Pals read familiar bedtime stories, Leap With Alice will allow those stories to leap off of the page and come to life! While the stories are read, Bluebee’s mouth and head move seamlessly to the words as if it were actually telling the story. Meanwhile, viewing the book from any smart device, Leap With Alice enhances each page. Readers can even integrate an avatar of themselves into their favorite books. The words spoken by a Bluebee Pal are emphasized through augmented reality, promoting higher retention and understanding.

This new partnership has opened the door to new relationships within the education community. We had the pleasure of visiting the Princeton House Charter School, meeting an inspirational group of educators and engaging students with unique learning abilities. Dedicated to children with Autism, Princeton House’s teachers are determined to apply creative forms of technology in their classrooms. Teachers and therapists collaborate on the various applications to integrate modern technology into their curriculum, social activities, and speech therapy sessions. Forming an immediate connection, Leap With Alice has partnered with Princeton House Charter School, built on a common vision of how technology can maximize every child’s potential.

Building bridges locally, the Leap With Alice team has become a pillar in the Orlando tech community. Last week, our CCO, Zack Pierola, presented at the Orlando Tech Association with the entire team answering questions, breaking down our mission and vision. On Wednesday, Victor Romero, our Lead Advisor and CEO of Miami Crypto Exchange, presented at 1 Million Cups and the Leap With Alice team was there in full support. It was a great opportunity to educate the entrepreneurs of Orlando on blockchain and cryptocurrency, as well as the impact that Security Tokens will have on the future of the stock market.

Leap With Alice is working with Miami Crypto Exchange to set the standard for future token offerings. Using their custom software to govern our token sale, the LWA token sale operates under Regulation D, Rule 506(c). In addition, our our Anti-Money Laundering program and Know Your Customers process meet the requirements of 31 C.F.R.s. 1022.210. and are correctly approved.

In recent months, this has become invaluable to any company exploring a token offering. Just this week, a seemingly legitimate ICO project — ShipChain — was issued a cease and desist letter by South Carolina for illegally selling securities in the state. The structure of the LWA Token Sale and regulatory governance gives investors peace of mind when dipping their toe into the cryptocurrency space, knowing that each LWA token has been distributed under complete compliance.

Among the first companies in history to tokenize its units of equity, our team is proud to be part of the movement to create the “Stock Market 2.0”. Stay tuned, there is much more to come!
