Orlando Is At the Edge of the Innovation Rabbit Hole

“We keep moving forward, opening new doors, and doing new things, because we’re curious and curiosity keeps leading us down new paths.”
-Walt Disney

Shattering preconceived notions, Orlando, Florida has evolved into much more than a theme park destination. In recent years, this emerging powerhouse of tech has lured major companies away from established hubs, such as Silicon Valley. Orlando was recently ranked as one of the Top 20 cities in the United States for tech growth, as well as being listed as one of the nine cities in line to be “the next Silicon Valley”.

“Orlando is a region of strong partnerships, scientific exploration and discovery, and creative development. It is a city known around the world. A city where people can build their dreams on a solid foundation of resources and community support.”

-Orlando Economic Partnership

Founded in Orlando, Leap With Alice is establishing a deep connection locally, propelling the city’s innovative growth. An impactful partnership with The University of Central Florida allows us to facilitate research studies, showcasing the efficacy of augmented reality in the classroom. Committed to giving back, we’ve pledged a $1M endowment, paving the way for continuous research and development through the university.

“Innovative technologies, such as Leap With Alice and the use of Augmented Reality, will have tremendous impact in the way students experience education, as well as the future employability for all programs, including STEM.”

-Dr. Trey Vasquez, Associate Professor, Child, Family, and Community Sciences Exceptional Education Program

Expanding our technological reach, Leap With Alice has established a strong partnership with Mercury Cash and The Miami Crypto Exchange; two companies focused on the cryptocurrency space and founded in Orlando. Together, our teams will create the standard for future Security Token Offerings (STO’s).

“A new market is being born, the Stock Market 2.0. Tokenizing traditional markets, ICOs will be replaced by Security Tokens Offerings (STOs) — a market expected to reach $10 Trillion by 2020. Miami Crypto Exchange will become the first globally compliant marketplace for Security and Non-Security Tokens. As a proud advisor to Leap With Alice, I look forward to the journey of enhancing our education system, as well as setting the new standard for future token offerings.”

-Victor Romero, CEO Mercury Cash, Miami Crypto Exchange

Tying the Central Florida community even closer together, Leap With Alice has also teamed up with Chatter Buzz — a local digital marketing agency — for marketing needs, recognizing the opportunity to expand globally while focusing locally.

“We all have different specialties, but we have the same values of collaboration and innovation to move Central Florida’s business community forward.”

-Shalyn Dever, CEO Chatter Buzz and Orlando Business Journal’s “40 Under 40” Honoree

Building the next technologically driven city is a community effort and expands beyond strategic partnerships. Leap With Alice is a dedicated supporter of entrepreneurship, fostering the growth of local meetups, such as 1 Million Cups and The Orlando Tech Association. Bringing the tech community together, these events are vital in cultivating sustainable growth within the city.

Our goal is to provide a tool for passionate educators — serving as every “Teacher’s Aide”. Reimagining the way we conduct education, Leap With Alice is empowering educators with the ability to target unique learning abilities, such as dyslexia or ADHD. Through personalized and engaging content, individual strengths are harnessed, showcasing every student’s educational superpower. We envision a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination.

The free creation suite (Alice Labs™) allows educators to bring everyday lesson plans to life, transforming the classroom from a passive work environment into an immersive learning experience through augmented reality. Just as easily as today’s social media applications allow you to augment your daily life, our tool provides simple features for enhancing the learning experience. Original content can be bought and sold within the Alice Exchange™, finally giving educators the means to receive compensation that properly reflects the monumental role they play in education.

Leveraging the innovative capabilities of augmented reality and blockchain technology, Leap With Alice is leading the way toward an educational revolution that is long overdue. Welcome, to education’s new reality. Come Leap With Alice!
