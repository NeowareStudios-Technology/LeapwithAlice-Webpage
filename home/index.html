A Leap In Creative Education

Leap With Alice empowers educators on a global scale, utilizing emerging technologies to reimagine the outdated education system. Providing a variety of EdTech tools, Leap With Alice enhances education with augmented reality, secures users with blockchain technology, and incentivizes activity through cryptocurrency. Leap With Alice creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination.



The Alice Suite

Leap With Alice provides a variety of applications and tools that allow educators to create, buy,and sell enhanced educational material. The AliceSuite is the culmination of these features.

AliceLabs

Easily create free educational content, enhanced by AR.

AliceLens

View augmented creations and rediscover the world.

AliceExchange

Sell creations on an incentive driven Educator-to-Educator marketplace.

AliceClassroom

Dashboard detailing KPI's such as student growth and engagement.

Leap With Alice & UCF

Leap With Alice is proud to announce our partnership with the University of Central Florida. Working together to create a deeper connection within the education community, Leap With Alice offices are located within the Toni Jennings Exceptional Education Institute. This partnership will allow Leap With Alice to conduct impactful studies, collecting data to strategically implement our technology and better the future of education.

“Innovative technology, such as Leap With Alice and the use of Augmented Reality, really have a tremendous promise in order to impact the way in which students will conduct education as well as there future employability for all programs, including STEM.”

-Dr. Trey Vasquez of UCF Toni Jennings Exceptional Education Institute
