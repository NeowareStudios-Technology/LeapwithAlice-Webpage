How to Energize Students and Incentivize Educators

Alice Labs™, The passionate educator’s free tool for creating, buying, and selling educational content, enhanced with Augmented Reality.

ORLANDO, USA and FARO, Portugal, July 25, 2018 -- Developing creative solutions to the issues that have plagued modern-day educators, EdTech innovator Leap With Alice has formally partnered with Portugal’s University of Algarve, working with their MILAGE Project and the MILAGE LEARN+ app. This partnership will deliver free educational resources across the globe, allowing educators to create powerful content that has been enhanced by emerging technology (such as augmented reality and machine learning).

"Leap With Alice is thrilled to partner with the University of Algarve and collaborate with the MILAGE LEARN+ platform in an effort to empower educators on a global scale. This marks the beginning of a global ecosystem of incentive-driven learning that allows educators to thrive in every region."
-Caesar Medel, Leap With Alice CEO and Co-Founder

The MILAGE Project has built a community of partners, including universities and schools located in Norway, Spain, Turkey, and Portugal. This developed network of educators and students will be the first to engage Leap With Alice’s free creation tools; enhancing content and cultivating a more immersive learning experience. The MILAGE LEARN+ platform is building a community of teachers that create content according to their specific needs, which is made available (for free) to all students. The University of Algarve is launching pilot projects with schools in the United States, using Portuguese and Spanish content for the learning of second languages, as well as training teachers in the use of the MILAGE LEARN+ platform to create new content for US curriculum.

"The partnership between Leap With Alice and the MILAGE LEARN+ platform provides an educational ecosystem to include all teachers and all students in an engaging, autonomous and active learning environment to foster 21-century challenges."
-Mauro Figueiredo, Instituto Superior de Engenharia - University of Algarve


About Leap With Alice:

Leap With Alice creates innovative solutions for educators by utilizing emerging technologies, such as augmented reality and blockchain technology. The AliceSuite™ encompasses a variety of tools, centered around AliceLabs™ -- a free creation platform that empowers educators with the ability to create AR-enhanced content. Simple drag-and-drop features and templates allow educators to create interactive content that increases engagement, resulting in a more effective learning experience. The AliceExchange™ facilitates an Educator-to-Educator marketplace with the capacity to buy and sell enhanced educational content. Leap With Alice creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination.

About Milage Project:

The MILAGE LEARN + free app was developed to enhance learning for all students using mobile devices. It implements a pedagogical model that includes gamification to motivate students. It promotes a more interactive, student-centered learning environment with diversified materials and feedback, including educational videos for all students. The MILAGE LEARN+ app also promotes students autonomy through a scheme of self and peer assessment. With the MILAGE LEARN+ app, all students have access to a common knowledge base of quality.

The free application MILAGE LEARN+ TEACHERS is also available for creating educational content and follow the work of students. For access, first register using this application: https://bit.ly/2uMlgiT
