Following the Money

“If I made a commitment, I stood by that commitment — and try to make it real. Because when you become leaders, the most important thing you have is your word, your trust. That’s where respect comes from.”
-Michelle Obama

When it comes to building a community around education, nothing is more important than trust. Parents trust that when they send their children to school that they are receiving a proper education. They trust that students are in a safe environment that promotes growth. Leap With Alice must foster this trust in order to be successful.

That ring of trust doesn’t just relate to education, it extends to potential investors and future holders of ALICE token. The world of Initial Coin Offerings has seen dishonorable intent, disguised by incredible ideas and crafty marketing. Sifting through the wide variety of token offerings is a daunting task, accompanied by many risks. Trust and transparency cannot be overlooked, in this regard. As we move forward, Leap With Alice will continue building higher levels of confidence by operating as an open book. In this chapter, we explore exactly where we plan to deploy our funding.

Leap With Alice was founded in January of 2017. Over the last 16 months, it has been a productive grind. We are proud to have released a Demo App on iOS and Android, giving everyone a tangible understanding of how Leap With Alice can impact the classroom. We’ve established deeply impactful partnerships, opening the door to research studies; bettering the future of education. All of this has been powered by sweat equity. A founding team with incredible passion and dedication.

Despite the meaningful progress, there is a long road ahead. We have been incredibly fortunate to receive interest from groups that wish to see the journey continue. As we seek the right match in our community, we are not looking for a quick transaction. Leap With Alice is dedicated to finding the right fit, just as much as an investor searches for the right project. Passion is what drives us and we want to share that passion with anyone who joins the Leap With Alice community. It is of the utmost importance that we all know exactly where funding has originated from and who we are welcoming into our community.

There are three key aspects to our ICO preparation: structure, legal, and marketing. You could argue that one is no more important than the other. However, we believe that without any one of the three, success is unlikely. Leap With Alice is currently working with a reputable ICO “turnkey” service provider that will build the ICO on a technical level (tokenomics, smart contracts), as well as bolstering the marketing reach of our token sale. As we navigate through this ambiguous regulatory climate, quality legal guidance is invaluable. These measures ensure Leap With Alice completes a successful token sale, while remaining perfectly compliant within all regulatory bodies.

The growth of the operational team will be the first major step in meeting the demand of a global vision. The advent of the ICO allows emerging companies to scale in a short period of time. Leap With Alice will be ready to impact classrooms worldwide by leveraging strategic partnerships and creating a robust team of dedicated professionals, skilled developers, and passionate educators.

As the Leap With Alice team grows, so will the list of milestones. Unlike most ICO startups, Leap With Alice provides tangible proof of progress. Building off of the release of the Demo App (prior to the Pre-ICO launch), we will release a beta version of the creation suite, or “Alice Labs”, as we launch the official ICO. This is where the real magic happens. Educators can begin augmenting their lesson plans, just as easily as they would use augmented reality to enhance their Snapchat. Simple drag-and-drop tools will allow educators across the world to create deeply engaging learning experiences.

After the close of the token sale, there is no shortage of work left to be done. Contributors to the Leap With Alice community will become more important than ever, as we launch the Alice Exchange. Finishing what our current Demo App of the Alice Lens has initiated, the Alice Exchange ties together each facet of Leap With Alice. Once a user has created a uniquely enhanced worksheet in Alice Labs, content creators will be able to sell their educational content in a P2P marketplace. Any educator may then purchase these lesson plans for their classrooms — which are brought to life, thanks to the Alice Lens.

This is only the beginning. The launch of a full educational tool box opens the door to a new realm of possibilities, allowing us to begin thinking outside of the book. It is our mission to ensure that every educator has the tool that lets them reimagine education and personalize each learning experience. Just as we entered with transparent intentions, we will continue to execute in the same manner, after the token sale ends. The journey will not be complete until educators of all nations, developed and undeveloped, are finally equipped with every resource they require.
