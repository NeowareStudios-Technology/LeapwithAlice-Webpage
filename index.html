This is
Hello Alice

We are a team focused on turning education sideways. We strive to innovate, enhance and improve what education can and should be.

Throught he use of Augmented Reality we make the old forms of education come to life. Planets literally jump out of a piece of paper and bring a whole new meaning to hands on learning. But how is this content being created? Well its pretty simple actually, it's you. We want to enable educators the oppurtunity to create lesson plans through Hello Alice. Once created we will give educators the chance to sell their lesson plans to other educators all accros the planet. Education is a collaborative effort. It may take a village to raise a child, but it takes the world to educate them.

In a world where education is one of the top priorities, we do not reward those on the ground level, the educators. Through our educators marketplace we strive to give educators the oppurtunity to sell their lesson plans to make extra income.
