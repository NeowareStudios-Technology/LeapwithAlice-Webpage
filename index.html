What is

Leap With Alice

Alice creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination. Welcome, to educations new reality, come take a Leap With Alice!

Leap With Alice provides a FREE Augmented Reality creation suite, giving educators the ability to reimagine how students learn. This FREE creation tool (‘Alice Labs’), represents a leap in creative education that transforms the classroom from a passive work environment into an immersive learning experience. Simple drag-and-drop tools and templates present teachers the resources needed to generate interactive content that increases engagement, resulting in a higher level of education.

The ‘Alice Exchange’ provides the capacity to create, buy, and sell enhanced educational content. The ALICE utility token acts as the native currency for the Alice Exchange, as well as an incentive structure, rewarding the creation and curation of quality content. Pairing AR with blockchain technology, Leap With Alice fosters a secure ecosystem where each user’s content, data, and funds are safely stored. Bringing the puzzle pieces together, Leap With Alice creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination.

How Leap With Alice

Redefines the Classroom

A Tool for All Educators

Students are immersed into lessons using the power of AR. Our free software allows educators to target individual student needs, especially those with unique learning abilities.

Authenticating Quality

Content is analyzed for plagiarism before reaching the Alice Exchange using AI and Machine Learning. A Review Board with specialists in the fields of Integrated STEM, Transdisciplinary STEM, Special Ed, Math, Science, Social Studies, and Literacy ensure quality.

Incentivizing Educators

Teachers have the ability to sell their personally created lessons on the Alice Exchange for Tokens. Alice Tokens can be cashed in for a payout, or used to purchase other creators lessons.

Leap With Alice & Bluebee Pals

Bluebee Pals are a Bluetooth enabled interactive plush learning companions linked to a FREE education app and pair with all apps with a narrative providing endless education and therapy. Educators, Occupational, ABA and Speech Therapists utilize Bluebee Pals as an effective motivational tool to engage in communication and learning. Bluebee Pals participate in repeating words, answering questions, and reading stories. Leap With Alice is partnering with Bluebee Pals to enhance engagement, in and beyond the classroom. Working together, we aim to create an assistive technology that peaks every child’s interest on an emotional and intellectual level.

As Bluebee Pals read familiar bedtime stories, Leap With Alice will allow those stories to leap off of the page and come to life. Each Bluebee’s mouth and head move while reading stories. Meanwhile, viewing the book from any smart device, Leap With Alice enhances each page. The words spoken by a Bluebee Pal are emphasized through augmented reality, promoting higher retention and understanding.

Dual Token Model

Adapting to the rapidly evolving landscape of the cryptocurrency space,
Leap With Alice will offer two tokens. This structure provides a unique balance of participation
in community growth and an equity ownership stake in Leap With Alice as a company.

LWA Token Distribution



The LWA token represents tokenized equity, where the ownerships grants anticipation of future profits in the form of dividends, revenue share and/or price appreciation. The LWA token sale serves as a mechanism to further continued development of the platform.

Company Allocation of Funds



Funds raised from the LWA token sale will be deployed with the immediate goal of acquiring high level talent, propelling the growth of our technology. Marketing will also play a pivotal role in building a massive community of educators across the globe. In addition, a strong legal team will ensure that Leap With Alice remains compliant in all phases of development.

Alice Token Distribution



The ALICE utility token serves as the native currency of the Alice Exchange, providing users a larger percentage of sales vs. fiat, while acting as an incentive for the creation and curation of content. This token will be released at a future date, coinciding with the release of a fully operational platform.
