This is
Alice

We are a team focused on turning education sideways. We strive to innovate, enhance and improve what education can and should be.

Through the use of Augmented Reality, we bring old forms of education to life. The solar system literally jumps out of a piece of paper and bring a whole new meaning to hands-on learning. But how is this content being created? Well its pretty simple actually, it's you. We want to enable educators with the opportunity to create lesson plans through Alice. Once created we give the opportunity to sell those lesson plans to others all across the world. Education is a collaborative effort. It may take a village to raise a child, but it takes the world to educate them.

Use Cases

The following use cases shine a spotlight on what is possible through the Leap with Alice platform. Educators will be given the tools to create immersive augmented reality experiences with ease.

Whether it is teaching sight word by seeing them in action, bringing the solar system to life in front of a class full of students, or even playing videos off of stationary material.

The limits of what can be created are all within the imagination of the creator.
