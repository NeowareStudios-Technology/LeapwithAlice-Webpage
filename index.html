What is

Leap With Alice

We’ve built an education tool that redefines the classroom.

Leap With Alice is a platform that creates engagement, builds learning, and incentivizes teachers.

Orchestrating a revolution of the outdated education system, Leap With Alice presents a platform and an exchange with the capacity to create, buy, and sell enhanced educational content. Through the use of Augmented Reality, we bring old forms of education to life. Pairing AR with Blockchain technology, LWA incentivizes the creation of quality content in a secure ecosystem where each user’s content, data, and funds are safely stored. Simple drag-and-drop tools and templates present teachers the resources needed to generate interactive content that increases engagement, resulting in a higher level of education.

How Leap With Alice

Redefines the Classroom

A Tool for All Students

Students are immersed into lessons using the power of Augmented Reality. Our software allows educators to target individual student needs, especially those with unique learning abilities.

Incentivising Educators

Teachers have the ability to sell their personally created lessons on the Alice Exchange for Tokens. Alice Tokens can be cashed in for a payout, or used to purchase other creators lessons.

Creating Security Within Ed-Tech

Utilizing Blockchain technology, Alice not only secures classrooms personal information, but educators intellectual property and earnings.

Pre-Sale & Values

  • - Utilized for the execution of smart contracts.
  • - Used to represent transferable value for buying/selling on the Alice Exchange.
  • - Awarded for quality contributions to the platform.
  • - Given to promote engagement with third party advertisers.

What is Leap With Alice?

LWA is a platform that provides content creators tools to enhance educational material with augmented reality. This content can be exchanged via the P2P Alice Exchange by utilizing the ALICE token.

What is the Alice Exchange?

The Alice Exchange is a venue for content creators and consumers to come together in a P2P environment that eliminates friction and incentivizes quality contributions to the LWA community.

What are the benefits of smart contract based account?

Distributed Ledger Technology allows for unprecedented levels of transparency, audit ability and transferability. LWA Accounts are built on an Ethereum smart contract, making them among the most secure available.

How is LWA different than a platform like Teachers Pay Teachers?

The ALICE token allows for an incentivization layer which rewards users for making positive contributions to the platform. Users have the opportunity to earn ALICE tokens by creating quality content, curating the content within the LWA platform, and engaging in third party advertisements.

What problems does LWA solve?

The combination of blockchain technology and augmented reality allows the LWA platform to generate solutions to the following issues with today’s educational system:

  • - Disengaged and disinterested students
  • - Content that ignores learning disabilities
  • - Unacceptable compensation for the value educators provide
  • - Lack of credibility for online certifications
  • - Securing personal and professional data

How is my account information protected?

Blockchain technology allows LWA to establish a secure ecosystem that allows each user’s content, data, and funds to be safely stored using Identity-Aware Proxy and multi-factor authentication.

What Cryptocurrencies can be used to purchase ALICE tokens?

ETH will be accepted for purchases.

How can I participate in the ICO Token Sale?

By sending ETH to our ALICE wallet, that will be posted during the Pre-ICO.

What will the token sale proceeds be used for?

The proceeds will be used to fund operations, development, marketing and legal departments.

Will there be a lockup period for ALICE tokens sold in the token sale?

No, ALICE tokens sold in the token sale will immediately be liquid.

Does LWA accept Fiat currency?

Not at this time.

Why have an ALICE token?

The reason for having the ALICE Token is simple. Incentivize participation to improve the underlying processes in the outdated Education and E-Learning industries by leveraging Blockchain technology in order to bring engaging and interactive content via the ALICE Exchange to students across the globe.

What is the utility of the ALICE token?

The Alice token is used for the following:

  • - Required to utilize the LWA platform and its tools.
  • - Provides functionality for the execution of smart contracts.
  • - Represents transferable value for buying/selling on the Alice Exchange.
  • - Incentivizes quality contributions to the platform.
  • - Rewards interactive engagement with third party advertisers.

What is the total supply of ALICE tokens?

The Alice token supply is capped at 90,000,000 tokens.

What is a blockchain?

The Blockchain is an electronic public ledger which records digital registry and asset transactions. The Blockchain can also be used as a ledger for audit and tracking progress. The Blockchain is a very secure tool leveraging encryption keys, algorithms and electronic wallets. As a registry, the Blockchain is the next generation technology that ultimately all retailers, financial institutions, government agencies, and business will utilize to support their operations.

Smart Contracts can...

  • - Function as 'multi-signature' accounts, so that funds are spent only when a required percentage of people agree
  • - Manage agreements between users, say, if one buys insurance from the other
  • - Provide utility to other contracts (similar to how a software library works)
  • - Store information about an application, such as domain registration information or membership records.

On what blockchain is the technology built?

The LWA smart contract operates on the Ethereum Blockchain and data is recorded on its native blockchain.

What is Augmented Reality?

A technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view.

What is a Smart Contract?

Standard contracts outline terms of a relationship (typically enforced by law), while a smart contract enforces a relationship with cryptographic code. These smart contracts are designed to execute terms precisely as they are set up by their creators.

What is the LWA Wallet?

Similar to a traditional wallet that you carry in your pocket, a blockchain asset wallet is used to store digital assets. The difference is that instead of storing a collection of bills and cards, a blockchain asset wallet stores a collection of private keys.
