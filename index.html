What is

Leap With Alice

Alice creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination. Welcome, to educations new reality, come take a Leap With Alice!

Leap With Alice provides a FREE Augmented Reality creation suite, giving educators the ability to reimagine how students learn. This FREE creation tool (‘Alice Labs’), represents a leap in creative education that transforms the classroom from a passive work environment into an immersive learning experience. Simple drag-and-drop tools and templates present teachers the resources needed to generate interactive content that increases engagement, resulting in a higher level of education.

The ‘Alice Exchange’ provides the capacity to create, buy, and sell enhanced educational content. The ALICE utility token acts as the native currency for the Alice Exchange, as well as an incentive structure, rewarding the creation and curation of quality content. Pairing AR with blockchain technology, Leap With Alice fosters a secure ecosystem where each user’s content, data, and funds are safely stored. Bringing the puzzle pieces together, Leap With Alice creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination.

How Leap With Alice

Redefines the Classroom

A Tool for All Educators

Students are immersed into lessons using the power of AR. Our free software allows educators to target individual student needs, especially those with unique learning abilities.

Authenticating Quality

Content is analyzed for plagiarism before reaching the Alice Exchange using AI and Machine Learning. A Review Board with specialists in the fields of Integrated STEM, Transdisciplinary STEM, Special Ed, Math, Science, Social Studies, and Literacy ensure quality.

Incentivizing Educators

Teachers have the ability to sell their personally created lessons on the Alice Exchange for Tokens. Alice Tokens can be cashed in for a payout, or used to purchase other creators lessons.

Dual Token Model

Adapting to the rapidly evolving landscape of the cryptocurrency space,
Leap With Alice will offer two tokens. This structure provides a unique balance of participation
in community growth and an equity ownership stake in Leap With Alice as a company.

LWA Token Distribution



The LWA token represents tokenized equity, where the ownerships grants anticipation of future profits in the form of dividends, revenue share and/or price appreciation. The LWA token sale serves as a mechanism to further continued development of the platform.

Company Allocation of Funds



Funds raised from the LWA token sale will be deployed with the immediate goal of acquiring high level talent, propelling the growth of our technology. Marketing will also play a pivotal role in building a massive community of educators across the globe. In addition, a strong legal team will ensure that Leap With Alice remains compliant in all phases of development.

Alice Token Distribution



The ALICE utility token serves as the native currency of the Alice Exchange, providing users a larger percentage of sales vs. fiat, while acting as an incentive for the creation and curation of content. This token will be released at a future date, coinciding with the release of a fully operational platform.

What is Leap With Alice?

Leap With Alice provides content creators tools to enhance educational material with augmented reality. This content can be exchanged via the P2P Alice Exchange by utilizing the ALICE token.

What is the Alice Exchange?

The Alice Exchange is a venue for content creators and consumers to come together in a P2P environment that eliminates friction and incentivizes quality contributions to the Leap With Alice community.

What are the benefits of smart contract based accounts?

Distributed Ledger Technology allows for unprecedented levels of transparency, audit ability and transferability. Leap With Alice Accounts are built on an Ethereum smart contract, making them among the most secure available.

How is my account information protected?

Blockchain technology allows Leap With Alice to establish a secure ecosystem that allows each user’s content, data, and funds to be safely stored using Identity-Aware Proxy and multi-factor authentication.

What problems does Leap With Alice solve?

The combination of blockchain technology and augmented reality allows the LWA platform to generate solutions to the following issues with today’s educational system:
- Disengaged and disinterested students
- Content that ignores learning disabilities
- Unacceptable compensation for the value educators provide
- Lack of credibility for online certifications
- Securing personal and professional data

How is Leap With Alice different than a platform like Teachers Pay Teachers?

Solving the major issues that have plagued existing educational marketplaces, Leap With Alice will implement multiple layers of quality control - eliminating plagiarism and ensuring quality. This is done through the use of AI/Machine Learning, an Education Board that oversees quality, and users that are incentivized to rate and review content. Further setting ourselves apart, users have the opportunity to earn ALICE tokens by creating quality content, curating the content within the Leap With Alice platform, and engaging in third party advertisements.

What is the LWA Token?

The LWA token is designed to represent a tokenized security, where the ownerships grants anticipation of future profits in the form of dividends, revenue share and/or price appreciation. In regards to the token sale, the LWA token will be available as a means to raise funds to further the develpment of the platform.

What is the ALICE Token?

The ALICE token serves as a utility token within Leap With Alice. It is used for the following:
- Eliminates transaction fees.
- Returns a higher percentage of sales, compared to Fiat.
- Facilitates the execution of smart contracts.
- Represents transferable value for buying/selling on the Alice Exchange.
- Incentivizes quality contributions throughout the experience.

What is a blockchain?

The Blockchain is an electronic public ledger which records digital registry and asset transactions. The Blockchain can also be used as a ledger for audit and tracking progress. The Blockchain is a very secure tool leveraging encryption keys, algorithms and electronic wallets. As a registry, the Blockchain is the next generation technology that ultimately all retailers, financial institutions, government agencies, and business will utilize to support their operations.

What can smart contracts do?

- Function as 'multi-signature' accounts, so that funds are spent only when a required percentage of people agree
- Manage agreements between users, say, if one buys insurance from the other
- Provide utility to other contracts (similar to how a software library works)
- Store information about an application, such as domain registration information or membership records.

On what blockchain is the technology built?

The Leap With Alice smart contract operates on the Ethereum Blockchain and data is recorded on its native blockchain.

What is Augmented Reality?

A technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view.

What is a Smart Contract?

Standard contracts outline terms of a relationship (typically enforced by law), while a smart contract enforces a relationship with cryptographic code. These smart contracts are designed to execute terms precisely as they are set up by their creators.

What is the LWA Wallet?

Similar to a traditional wallet that you carry in your pocket, a blockchain asset wallet is used to store digital assets. The difference is that instead of storing a collection of bills and cards, a blockchain asset wallet stores a collection of private keys.
