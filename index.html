A Leap In Creative Education

ALICE leverages emerging technologies to reimagine how we view education. Providing a variety of FREE EdTech tools, ALICE brings augmented reality to any learning environment while empowering content creators with an innovative Digital Content Management system. ALICE creates a world where students learn through innovation and educators earn through imagination.

ALICE & UCF

ALICE is proud to announce our partnership with the University of Central Florida. Working together to create a deeper connection within the education community, ALICE offices are located within the Toni Jennings Exceptional Education Institute. This partnership will allow ALICE to conduct impactful studies, collecting data to strategically implement our technology and better the future of education.

“Innovative technology, such as ALICE and the use of Augmented Reality, really have a tremendous promise in order to impact the way in which students will conduct education as well as there future employability for all programs, including STEM.”

-Dr. Trey Vasquez of UCF Toni Jennings Exceptional Education Institute

ALICE & Amazing Explorers

Amazing Explorers Academy fosters a nurturing environment where little minds open up to develop lifelong learning skills designed to inspire, connect and engage the great minds of the 21st century.

ALICE will partner with Amazing Explorers Academy to take their hands-on learning approach to new heights.
