Private Pre-Sale: How to Participate

The countdown to the Leap With Alice Private Pre-Sale has begun, as we are now under five days away from the launch. We’ve been incredibly fortunate to have such strong support from our family and friends during this exciting period of development! The community is growing larger every day and internally, we have been swamped with interested individuals seeking to be a part of our Private Pre-Sale. We love the enthusiastic outreach via Email, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram, and even a good old fashioned phone call or two!

So many exciting events have transpired in recent weeks. We previously announced our partnership with the largest university within the United States, the University of Central Florida. Through this partnership, Leap With Alice will facilitate research studies, showcasing the efficacy of AR in the classroom — including studies conducted in association with the National Science Foundation. Leap With Alice offices are located on the campus, within the Toni Jennings Exceptional Education Institute.

“Innovative technology, such as Leap With Alice and the use of Augmented Reality, really have tremendous promise — impacting the way in which students will conduct education, as well as their future employability for all programs, including STEM.” -Dr. Trey Vasquez

Leap With Alice is grateful for the guidance of Mercury Cash and Miami Crypto Exchange — the industries first legal, fully compliant crypto gateway for security and non-security tokens. Working as Senior Advisor, CEO Victor Romero provides first hand experience and invaluable knowledge of the cryptocurrency space. Approaching the Private Pre-Sale of the LWA Token on May 15th (closed to , this guidance has provided the framework for the next generation of compliant token offerings.

“A new market is being born, the Stock Market 2.0. Tokenizing traditional markets, ICOs will be replaced by Security Tokens Offerings (STOs) — a market expected to reach $10 Trillion by 2020. Miami Crypto Exchange will become the first globally compliant marketplace for Security and Non-Security Tokens. As a proud advisor to Leap With Alice, I look forward to the journey of enhancing our education system, as well as setting the new standard for future token offerings.” -Victor Romero, CEO Mercury Cash, Miami Crypto Exchange.

This is only the beginning of our journey! The support has been overwhelming and we deeply apologize if we are unable to respond to Private Pre-Sale requests immediately. We do ask that everyone keep in mind that the Private Pre-Sale is just that, “Private”. At this stage, contributions are only accepted by direct friends and family of the Leap With Alice team. Further, we ask that everyone remain cautious, as Leap With Alice will never solicit investment. Our team is working diligently to ensure the safety of our community, thwarting false claims and fake accounts immediately.

The first question you should be asking yourself is “why do I want to participate?”. Leap With Alice seeks to attract friends and family who believe in our project long term.

For friends and family interested in joining the Private Pre-Sale, we encourage you to reach out directly to us. In order to increase your opportunity to receive a spot in the Private Pre-Sale, a direct connection will aide in the process. If you do not have a direct line, speak to a fellow friend that has already been welcomed into the Private Pre-Sale and have them connect us. The key for everyone to remember is that we are looking for value! We want to know how you can contribute, why our project interests you, and how you align with our vision.

Receiving a spot in this private pre-sale is an active commitment to making an impact on education. We cannot do it alone. We need you to join us as we reimagine education. Reach out to us and let us know exactly how you envision the future of education!
